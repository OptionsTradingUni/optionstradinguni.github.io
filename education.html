Modules — Full Curriculum

MODULE 1: OPTIONS TRADING 101

Lesson 1 — What Is An Option? — Options give the right, not obligation, to buy (Call) or sell (Put) at a strike before expiration.

Lesson 2 — Option Anatomy — Strike, expiration, premium, bid/ask, implied volatility, Greeks (Delta, Theta, Gamma, Vega).

Lesson 3 — Why Trade Options? — Leverage, defined risk (buyers), strategy flexibility (spreads, iron condors, covered calls).

MODULE 2: GETTING STARTED TRADING

Platforms: Webull, TradingView, Robinhood, thinkorswim.

Order types: Limit, market, stop, OCO.

Choosing contracts: liquidity, IV, open interest.

MODULE 3: READING THE CHART

Use candles, support/resistance zones, EMAs (10/20/50), volume confirmation, multi-timeframe analysis.

MODULE 4: BUILDING STRATEGIES

Trend trading setups, scalping for small accounts, vertical spreads, credit spreads, and journaling best practices.

MODULE 5: MINDSET & RISK

Discipline, cut losers, ride winners, avoid FOMO, risk 1–5% per trade, track your performance and taxes.

Glossary & Fintwit Slang

CALL — you want the stock to go up.
PUT — you want the stock to go down.
STRIKE PRICE — the price the option controls.
PREMIUM — the price you pay to buy the option.
EXPIRATION — contract deadline.
ITM / OTM / ATM — in/out/at the money.
GREEKS — Delta / Theta / Gamma / Vega.
OPEN INTEREST — contracts outstanding.
SWEEP — aggressive buy that hits many exchanges.
BLOCK — large order placed with less urgency.
LOTTO — cheap short-dated gamble.
FOMO — fear of missing out.
SCALP — quick in/out trade for small gains.
WICKED OUT — stopped by a wick spike.
ROLLING — moving position to a different strike/expiry.
ATH / ATL — all time high / low.
